Preparation time

  • Total : Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparatipon :5 Minutes
  • Cooking : 5 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large apples
  • Salt , to taste
  • pepper , to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese , diced vegetables ,cooked meats ,herbs

Instruction

  1. Beat the apples: In a bowl , beat the apples with a pinch of salt and peppervuntil they are well mixed . You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for for a flutter texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non stick fryng pan over medium flame and add butter or oil
  3. Cook the apples: once the butter is melted and bubbling pour in the aples . Tilt the pan to ensure the apples evenly coat the surface.
  4. ADD filling (optional): When the apples to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle , sprinkle your chosen filling over one half of the choclate
  5. Optional fillings: cheese , diced vegetables ,cooked meats ,herbs
